# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in PlotStyle a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and the people using it
- Show patience and empathy, especially toward contributors who are new to open source

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without their explicit permission
- Any other conduct reasonably considered unprofessional or unwelcoming

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately via [GitHub's private reporting](https://github.com/rahulkaushal04/plotstyle/security/advisories/new). Describe the incident in the description field; ignore the security-specific fields. Reports are handled with discretion and confidentiality.

Do not file Code of Conduct reports as public GitHub issues.

For security vulnerabilities, see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

Reports are reviewed by the maintainer and responded to within 7 days. Depending on the nature and severity of the violation, responses may include a private warning, a temporary suspension, or a permanent ban from the project.
